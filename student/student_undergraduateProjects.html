Smart India Hackathon

The AICTE in association with MHRD & NIC have planned to host SMART INDIA HACKATHON 2017, world biggest digital movement . The prime objective was to involvetechnology students in the digital revolution of India. The Government of India in association with29 ministries have proposed around 598 problem statements. The college team, consisting of 6 member each with atleast 1 female candidate, have to choose the problem statement and provide the solution.The Hackthon was a grand success with 36 Hrs non-stop digital product devlopment competition held on 1st and 2nd April, 2017. Around 40+ Thousand technology students and 2100+ technological institute participated in this grand hackathon.
